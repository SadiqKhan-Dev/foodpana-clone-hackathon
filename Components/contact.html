Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a foodpanda partner?

You can become a partner by visiting our Partners page and filling out the registration form. Our team will contact you within 2-3 business days.

What are your delivery hours?

Our delivery hours vary by location, but most areas have 24/7 service. Check the app for specific restaurant hours in your area.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time through the foodpanda app. You'll receive notifications at each stage of delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, cash on delivery, and various local payment methods depending on your location.

How can I apply for a rider position?

Visit our Careers page or download the rider app to apply. You'll need a valid driver's license and vehicle registration.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

Yes! We offer corporate accounts with special benefits. Contact our business team for more information.
