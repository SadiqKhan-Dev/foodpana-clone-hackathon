Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

Building a workplace where everyone belongs and can thrive.

Our Commitment

At foodpanda, we believe that diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) are fundamental to our success. We're committed to creating a workplace where all employees feel valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives and talents.

Our Approach

We embrace diversity in all its forms - from gender and ethnicity to age, sexual orientation, and background. With over 50 nationalities and 20 languages spoken across our markets, we leverage our diversity as a competitive advantage to better serve our global customer base.

Our commitment to equity ensures that all employees have equal access to opportunities, resources, and advancement. We continuously work to eliminate barriers and create an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.

Our DEI Initiatives

Employee Resource Groups

Supporting communities within our organization, including groups for women, LGBTQ+ employees, and various cultural associations.

Unconscious Bias Training

Providing education to all employees and managers to recognize and address unconscious bias in the workplace.

Equitable Hiring Practices

Implementing structured interviews, diverse hiring panels, and inclusive job descriptions to attract diverse talent.

Pay Equity Analysis

Regularly reviewing compensation data to ensure fair and equitable pay across all roles and demographics.
