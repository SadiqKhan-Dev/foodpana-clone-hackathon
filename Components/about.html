Our Story

foodpanda was founded in 2012 and is Asia's largest food and grocery delivery platform outside of China. Operating in more than 400 cities across 11 markets, we continue to expand and grow in our core food delivery business.

As part of Delivery Hero, a global leader in the food delivery industry, we process over 3 million orders every day and work with approximately 500,000+ restaurant partners.

We're not just about delivering food - we're about creating opportunities, supporting local businesses, and making life easier for millions of people across Asia.
