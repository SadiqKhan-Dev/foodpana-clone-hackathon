Our Purpose

At foodpanda, we believe that our success is measured not just by our business growth, but by the positive impact we create in the communities we serve. Our pandapurpose is our commitment to being a responsible corporate citizen and making a meaningful difference.

Our Core Pillars

Sustainable Operations

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through eco-friendly packaging, optimizing delivery routes, and supporting sustainable restaurants.

Community Support

We actively support local communities through food donations, disaster relief efforts, and partnerships with local organizations to create positive social impact.

Empowering Partners

We help our restaurant partners grow their businesses, support local economies, and provide opportunities for entrepreneurs to succeed.

Employee Well-being

We prioritize the safety, health, and development of our employees and riders, providing them with fair opportunities and support.

Creating Positive Impact

Through our pandapurpose initiative, we've made significant strides in creating positive change across our markets. From supporting local restaurants during challenging times to implementing sustainable practices, we're committed to doing business responsibly.

Our efforts include reducing single-use plastic packaging, supporting local farmers and suppliers, and creating employment opportunities for thousands of riders across Asia.

Our Key Initiatives

Green Delivery Program

Implementing eco-friendly delivery options and electric vehicles in select markets to reduce carbon emissions.

Restaurant Growth Program

Providing training and resources to help restaurant partners improve their operations and grow their businesses.

Digital Literacy Program

Helping small restaurants digitize their operations and reach more customers through our platform.

Community Kitchen

Partnering with local organizations to provide meals to those in need during emergencies and special occasions.
